About us...

Rescue The Wild (RTW), established in Canada in 2010, is an organization that aims to protect wildlife animals by raising awareness and to promote sustainable living. Over the past few decades, human activity has been pushing many animal species near extinction, losing about 10,000 species each year, in which current extinction rates are accelerating way faster than the previous five mass extinctions on Earth. We believe that local communities can create a sustainable future, where wildlife habitats will be protected alongside the betterment of human life. Our organization saves species that are on the brink of extinction by providing opportunities for scientists to gain better knowledge, restore wildlife habitats, and protect animals from pollution and natural hazards. By conserving wildlife, we can ensure the co-existance of all living things, while maintaining ecological balance, so it improves our overall quality of life.

What we do

  • We protect wildlife as it provides balance and stability to the world's ecosystems.
  • We maintain wildlife diversity
  • We educate people on the importance of wildlife and wildlife habitats, and how they enhance human life
  • We encourage people to make changes to their lifestyle to create a healthy future for ourselves and the environment
