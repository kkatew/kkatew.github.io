Want to get involved?

Make a change today!

Our everyday choices, including small ones, can have a significant impact on the environment due to our careless consumption of natural resources. You might be thinking that your choices may not possibly affect the planet, but the truth is, if all 7.5 billion of us are thinking the same way, the impact actually becomes enormous.

Here are some earth-friendly things that you could start doing today to help save the environment:

1

Use less plastic

Bring reusable bags when grocery shopping to reduce plastic waste.

2

Travel sustainably

Private vehicles are one of the main contributors to global warming, so choose to walk, cycle, take public transportation, or carpool as a way to reduce greenhouse gas emissions.

3

Reduce food waste

Only buy food you need to reduce your carbon footprint.
